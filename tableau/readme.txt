# Brazilian E-Commerce Data Analysis

This repository contains an analysis of the **Brazilian E-Commerce Public Dataset by Olist**, covering 100,000 orders from 2016 to 2018 across various marketplaces in Brazil. The data includes information on customer locations, orders, timestamps, and prices.

### Datasets Used
For this analysis, we utilized the following five datasets from the original dataset:
1. **Orders**
2. **Order Items**
3. **Products**
4. **Customers**
5. **Sellers**

Full dataset link: [Brazilian E-Commerce Dataset on Kaggle](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/olistbr/brazilian-ecommerce)

### Key Analytical Focus Areas
1. **Top Selling Regions**  
   *Objective*: Identify regions generating the highest revenue.  
   *Approach*: Aggregate and rank sales by state.  
   *Benefits*: Inform strategic marketing based on top-performing regions.

2. **Top Categories by Revenue**  
   *Objective*: Discover which product categories generate the most revenue.  
   *Approach*: Sum and rank total sales by category.  
   *Benefits*: Focus promotions on high-revenue categories.

3. **Top 5 Products by Revenue**  
   *Objective*: Find the five highest-earning products.  
   *Approach*: Calculate total revenue per product and rank the top five.  
   *Benefits*: Understand consumer preferences and market trends.

4. **Monthly Order Volume**  
   *Objective*: Analyze order volume trends to identify peak demand months.  
   *Approach*: Group orders by month and year.  
   *Benefits*: Improve revenue forecasting and optimize marketing campaign timing.
